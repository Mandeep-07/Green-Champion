Essential features

User-friendly interface

Role-tailored dashboards for citizens, waste workers and Green Champions — responsive, accessible and multilingual.

Training & certification

E-learning modules, quizzes, certificates and progress tracking to make training measurable and mandatory.

Geo-tagged reporting

Snap a photo, geo-tag it, escalate to area committees with status, photos and verification workflows.

Vehicle & route tracking

Real-time GPS integration, historical logs and route optimization for ULB fleets.

Incentives & penalties

Gamified leaderboards, rewards for compliant households and automated penalization for repeat non-compliance.

Facility locator & e-commerce

Find biomethanation, recycling centres and purchase kits/dustbins via an integrated shopping portal.

Workflow outline

  1. Registration & onboarding: OTP-based sign-up, role selection and directed onboarding.
  2. Training & certification: Videos, quizzes, practical badges and certificates.
  3. Utility distribution: Request dustbins/kits, track delivery, auto-reminders.
  4. Incident reporting: Upload geo-tagged photos; Green Champions verify & close.
  5. Compliance monitoring: Scheduled collection logs, segregation checks, automated exceptions.
  6. Incentive & engagement: Points, ranks, and rewards for households and buildings.
  7. Maps & tracking: Live vehicle feeds and facility maps for citizens and admins.
